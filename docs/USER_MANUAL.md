# 📖 User Manual - HMS Pro

Welcome to the Hospital Management System (HMS) Pro. This guide helps staff navigate the system based on their roles.

## 🏠 Dashboard
The first screen you see after login. It provides a real-time overview of hospital statistics including total patients, daily appointments, and pending reports.

## 👥 Patient Management
**For: Receptionists, Nurses, Doctors**
1. Navigate to the **Patients** tab.
2. Click **"Register New Patient"** to add a record.
3. Use the search bar to find existing patients by name or ID.
4. Click a patient to view their full medical history.

## 📅 Appointments
**For: Receptionists, Doctors, Patients**
- **Receptionists**: View the daily schedule and confirm pending appointments.
- **Doctors**: View your assigned patients for the day.
- **Patients**: Book a slot via the patient portal (optional module).

## 🩺 Clinical Workflow (EMR)
**For: Nurses & Doctors**
1. **Nurses**: Log vital signs (BP, Temp, Pulse) before the doctor visit.
2. **Doctors**: Open the consultation view, record symptoms/diagnosis, and write treatments.
3. **Prescriptions**: Linked directly to the Pharmacy for dispensing.

## 💊 Pharmacy & Inventory
**For: Pharmacists**
- View pending prescriptions from the Pharmacy tab.
- Click **"Dispense"** to fulfill orders (automatically updates inventory counts).
- Monitor drug stock levels in the Inventory sub-module.

## 🔐 Security & Logout
Always log out when leaving your terminal by clicking the **"Logout"** button at the bottom of the sidebar to protect patient data.
